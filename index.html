DOTA 2 CHARADES

Dota 2 charades is a WIP browser based guessing game in the (very) early stages of development.

The aim is to try to guess the names of as many Dota 2 ability icons and sounds as possible within the time limit!

Features coming in the near future:

    Single Player Experience: Play gainst the clock to achieve a high score.
  • Stopwatch timer!
  • Scoreboard!
  • Audio guesses!

Multiplayer gameplay will follow after the development of a solid single-player experience.
